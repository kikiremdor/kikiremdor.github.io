How to Take Screenshot on Windows 11 Keyboard#

Taking a screenshot on Windows 11 can be done without the need of an extra application. All you need is located conveniently within the keyboard shortcut of your Windows 11. With this knowledge, unlock the power of taking screenshots on your Windows 11 keyboard.

The first step is to press the Windows Key + Shift +S. This will activate the snipping tool and allow you to select the area of the screen you want to capture. Once you have the area you want captured, press Ctrl + Prt Sc or Prt Sc to capture the screenshot. The screenshot will then be saved to your clipboard that can easily be pasted into any image editor.

If you want to capture the entire screen, press the Windows Key + Prt Sc and a copy of the entire screen will be automatically saved to your screenshots folder in your Pictures library. All screenshots are named “Screenshot (number)” with the number indicating how many screenshots you have taken.

The final step is to press the “Windows Key + H” to open the Share Charm menu, where you can share the screenshot to your friends and family. By using the Windows 11 keyboard shortcuts for taking screenshots, you can easily capture what’s on your screen and share it with anyone.
