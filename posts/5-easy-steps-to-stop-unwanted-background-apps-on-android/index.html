1. Understand What Background Apps are#

Background apps are those apps that run in the background on our smartphones, and consume data even when we aren’t using them. Learning to stop these unwanted background apps can help you save your data and optimize your phone’s performance. Here are 5 easy steps to do it on an Android device. First, go to your phone’s settings and tap ‘Data Usage’. This will show you the data that’s used by each application. Find the apps that are using the most data and select them. Tap ‘Force stop’ to stop them from running and save data. Second, if you want to stop an app from running in the background, go to ‘Battery’ in Settings and tap on ‘Battery optimization’. You will see a list of all the applications installed on your phone. For any application you don’t want running in the background, tap on it and select ‘Don’t Optimize’. Third, you can also use ‘Data Saver’ in Settings to limit data usage. This will restrict how much data apps can use in the background. When enabled, only the apps you select will be allowed to use data in the background. Fourth, if you want to completely disable an app, go to ‘Apps’ in Settings and select the app you want to disable. Tap on it and then tap on ‘Uninstall’. Doing this will remove the app from your phone and stop it from running in the background. You can install the app again if you need to. These are the 5 easy steps to stop unwanted background apps on Android. They can help you save data and improve your phone’s performance.
