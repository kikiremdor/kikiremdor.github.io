Q1 : What is a QR code?

A1 : A QR code (abbreviated from Quick Response code) is a type of matrix barcode (or two-dimensional code) designed to be read by a scanner or camera. A QR code consists of black squares arranged in a square grid on a white background, which can be read by devices equipped with a camera such as a smartphone or tablet.
