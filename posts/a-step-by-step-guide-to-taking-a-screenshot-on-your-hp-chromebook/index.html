If you prefer to capture only an active window, press the Ctrl + Alt + Switch Window keys. This will take a screenshot of the window you are currently working on and will also be stored in the Downloads folder.

Finally, if you prefer to take a screenshot directly to your clipboard, press the Ctrl + Shift + Switch Window keys together. Doing so will copy the screenshot directly to your clipboard and you can then paste it wherever you wish.
