1. Introduction to Cookies and Cache#

Cookies and cache are temporary files stored on your computer to help the Chrome browser load websites faster. If they become too large or too many, it can cause slow page loading times. Clearing them can help improve Chrome mobile performance. This step-by-step guide will explain how to clear both cookies and cache on your Chrome browser.

First, open the Chrome app on your mobile device. In the bottom right corner, click on the three dot icon. This will open the Chrome menu. Scroll down and select Settings, then scroll down and select Privacy.

Next, you will need to click on Clear Browsing Data. This will bring up a window which will let you decide which types of data to delete. Make sure both cookies and cached images and files are checked. After selecting those options, click on Clear Data

Finally, restart the Chrome browser to ensure that the changes have taken effect. This will help to improve Chrome’s mobile performance. Keep in mind that cookies and cache can also be cleared manually from the browser. However, doing it through the Chrome menu is easier and more efficient.
