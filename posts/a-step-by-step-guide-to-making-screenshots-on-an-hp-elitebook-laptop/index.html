We’ll start by taking a look at the different methods of screenshot capturing available. On your EliteBook laptop, you have two different ways to take screenshots: using the Print Screen key or by using the Snipping Tool. The Print Screen key is usually located on the top row of your keyboard and captures a full-screen snapshot of whatever is currently displayed on your monitor. The Snipping Tool, on the other hand, can be found in the Windows Start Menu and allows you to select a specific area of the screen to capture. Now that we have an understanding of the two options, let’s take a look at how to make a screenshot on an HP EliteBook laptop.

The first step is to open the program or application whose screenshot you would like to capture. Now, depending on which method you want to use, press either the Print Screen key or open the Snipping Tool. If you used the Print Screen key, your screenshot will be saved as an image in your computer’s Pictures folder. If you’re using the Snipping Tool, all you need to do is select the area of your screen you want to capture, hit “Ctrl+ C” and your screenshot will be copied to your clipboard.

Now that you’ve taken your screenshot, you can share it with others. If you’ve saved it in the Pictures folder, you can easily attach it to an email or post it on an online message board. If you’ve copied it to the clipboard, just paste it into a Word document, an email, etc. Within a few seconds, you’ve gone from capturing your image to sharing it with the world!
