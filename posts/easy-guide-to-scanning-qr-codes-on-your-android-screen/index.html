1. What is a QR code?#

QR codes are quickly becoming a popular way to share information, whether it’s a web link, a text message, or even a business contact card. A QR code is a type of two-dimensional barcode that can be scanned using your Android smartphone’s camera. In this article, we’ll show you how to quickly and easily scan a QR code on your Android screen.

First, open up your camera app and point it at the QR code. Most modern Android phones will automatically detect the QR code and display a QR code detected message. If your phone doesn’t detect the QR code, open the settings menu in your camera app and look for an option to enable QR code scanning.

Once the QR code has been detected, your phone will provide you with information about the QR code along with a prompt to open the information. When you open the information, you’ll be taken to whatever page or service the QR code contains – whether it’s a web link, an app download page, or an email message.

Finally, you should be aware that not all QR codes are created equal. Some may contain malicious programs or viruses, so be sure to only scan QR codes from known and trusted sources. Scanning QR codes can be a great way to quickly access information on your Android device, but be sure to stay safe when doing so.
