2. Check Battery Level#

Knowing the current battery life of devices connected to your Android through Bluetooth can be useful. It’s a good idea to keep an eye on them to ensure they’ll continue working without interruption. Here’s how to check the Bluetooth battery level on Android.

First, locate and select the Bluetooth icon on your phone’s home screen or app list. Tap the menu icon at the top right corner of the Bluetooth settings. Once the menu opens, tap ‘Device battery level.’ This should display the current battery level for all the devices connected to your phone via Bluetooth.

If you don’t see the Device battery level option, tap ‘Advanced’ from the same menu section. This should open the list of options where you can find the ‘Device battery level’ option. Select it to view the battery level of your connected Bluetooth devices.

You can also check the battery life of your connected devices through the ‘Recently used devices’ option in the Bluetooth settings. This will show the time and percentage of the device’s remaining power.
