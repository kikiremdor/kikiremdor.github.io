1. Introduction to QR Codes#

QR codes are the black and white barcodes that can be found on websites, in magazines and newspapers, and even on physical products. When scanned with a compatible device, such as a smartphone, they can quickly take you to websites, provide product information, or even grant access to hidden features. This article will explain what QR codes are and how to scan them with an Android phone.

First, it is important to understand what QR codes are. QR stands for Quick Response and these codes are two dimensional barcodes that contain information. This information can range from a simple website link to detailed text messages.

Scanning a QR code is easy and can be done in a few simple steps. First, open your camera app and point it at the QR code. Then, the Android system will recognize the code and show an alert. You can then open the link or message contained in the code.

QR codes are a powerful tool for businesses and individuals alike. By scanning the codes, you can quickly access websites, product information, and more. With an Android smartphone, you’ll be able to scan any QR code with ease.
