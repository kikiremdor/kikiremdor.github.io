Next, select ‘Screenshots’ from the left side of this window. In here, you can manage the screenshots for each of your games. Select a folder for each game in which you want your screenshots to be saved.

Finally, you can also add a shortcut key for taking screenshots. From the Screenshots section of the settings, click on ‘Change shortcut’ and enter a new shortcut for taking screenshots. Once you have made all the changes, click on ‘OK’ to save your settings.
