Fourth, look at the battery temperature. This will tell you if your battery is over-heating, as this can lead to a significant decrease in performance and damage to the overall life of the battery.

Q2: How do I turn off background apps on my Android phone? A2: Turning off background apps on an Android phone can be done by going to Settings > Apps & Notifications > See All Apps > App Info > choose app > Background Data(toggle off).

Q3: How do I check for updates on my Android phone? A3: Checking for updates on an Android phone can be done by going to Settings > System > Advanced > System Update > Check for Update.

Q4: How do I view battery usage on my Android phone? A4: Viewing battery usage on an Android phone can be done by going to Settings > Battery > Battery Usage.

Q5: How can I reduce screen brightness/timeout on my Android phone? A5: Reducing screen brightness/timeout on an Android phone can be done by going to Settings > Display > Adaptive Brightness (toggle off) > Brightness Level (adjust manually).
