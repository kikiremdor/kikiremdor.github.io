1. Turning Off Your Android Flashlight#

If you’ve ever been out at night and needed to use the flashlight on your Android phone, you may have wondered how to turn it off again. Fortunately, most Android phones come with an inbuilt flashlight feature, and it’s easy to turn off. Here are the steps you need to take.

The first thing you need to do is open the Notification Shade on your phone. You can do this by swiping down from the top of your phone’s display screen. Once the shade is open, you should see a flashlight icon which looks like a circle with a lightning bolt in the middle.

Tap on the icon and the flashlight will turn off. If you can still see the flashlight icon, it means the flashlight is still on and you should tap the icon one more time to turn it off.

You can also turn off the flashlight by going into your phone’s settings. Go to “Settings” then “Display & Brightness” and you should find a toggle switch that says “Flashlight.” Tap this to turn off the flashlight.

Finally, some Android phones come with a “Smart Lock” feature. This feature can keep the flashlight on, even when you close the Notification Shade and even after you’ve restarted your phone. To disable this feature, go to “Settings” and then “Security” and turn off the Smart Lock.
