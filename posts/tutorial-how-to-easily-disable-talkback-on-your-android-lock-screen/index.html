- What is Talkback#

Talkback is an accessibility feature on Android devices that utilizes speech out loud technology to help people with vision impairments navigate their devices. It is activated by default when a user turns on their device, and it can easily be disabled. To disable Talkback, you will need to access the Accessibility Settings on your device. Once there, you will have the option to disable the feature with a simple tap. You may also have to turn off a few other settings in the Accessibility Settings menu, depending on your device. After doing so, your lock screen should be free of Talkback and you should be able to access it normally.
