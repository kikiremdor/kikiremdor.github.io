1. Activate Bluetooth on both devices#

Activating Bluetooth on two different devices is the first step in transferring photos from an iPhone to an Android device with Bluetooth. On your iPhone, swipe up from the home screen to open the Control Center and tap the Bluetooth icon to turn it on. Similarly, on your Android device, open the Settings app and tap the Bluetooth option to turn it on. Once both devices are connected, you will be able to pair them together.

Next, pair the two devices. On your iPhone, make sure you have turned on the Bluetooth and open the Files app. Tap the Bluetooth icon at the top right of the window. At the same time, you should see a prompt on your Android device. Tap “Pair” on both devices to establish a connection.

Once you have established a connection between the two devices, you can choose which files you want to transfer. On your iPhone, choose “Send” and select the photos you want to transfer. You can select multiple photos at once. On your Android device, choose “Connect” and select the same photos. You will see a prompt at the bottom of the screen when transfer is complete.

Finally, both devices will show a confirmation that the transfer is complete. On your Android device, you will find the photos in the Gallery app. On your iPhone, the transferred photos will be located in the Files app. With these simple steps, you can easily transfer photos from an iPhone to an Android device with Bluetooth.
