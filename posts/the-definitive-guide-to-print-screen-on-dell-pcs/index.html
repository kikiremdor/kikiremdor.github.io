1. Bagaimana cara menggunakan fitur Print Screen pada PC Dell dengan menggunakan keyboard#

Print screen is an incredibly useful tool for capturing screenshots on Dell PCs. Using your keyboard, you can quickly and easily take screenshots of your whole screen or just select sections of it. To do this, press the Print Screen key, which is typically located at the top right-hand side of your keyboard. This will snap a screenshot of your entire display, which can then be saved or printed. To capture a partial screenshot, press the Alt and Print Screen keys at the same time. This will capture only the active window you have open. All screenshots can be saved to the Pictures folder or another folder of your choice.

  1. Tekan tombol Print Screen (PrtScn) untuk menangkap gambar secara keseluruhan dari layar.

Q3: How can I save a Print Screen image? A3: After pressing the Print Screen key, the image can be saved by pressing the “Ctrl” and “V” keys at the same time. Q4: What other functions can Print Screen perform? A4: Print Screen can also be used to capture images from certain websites. Pressing the “Alt” and “Print Screen” keys at the same time will capture the currently selected window. This can be used to capture screenshots of webpages, software, or other data.
