The process of deleting emails in bulk can be done with a few simple steps. Here’s how to do it.

First, open the Gmail app on your Android device and tap on the ‘Menu’ icon in the top right corner. Select ‘Settings’ from the menu. On the Settings page, scroll down until you find the ‘Delete emails’ option. Tap on it to open.

Next, select ‘Bulk delete’ from the ‘Delete emails’ options. This will open the Bulk delete dialogue box. Here, you can select the emails you want to delete. You can choose to delete all emails, or only those emails that are older than a certain number of days.

Once you have selected the emails to delete, tap the ‘Delete’ button at the bottom of the dialogue box. All of the selected emails will be permanently deleted from your Android Gmail app. After deleting, the app will give you a confirmation message to let you know the process was successful.

By following these steps, you can easily delete Gmail emails in bulk and manage your inbox more effectively. This way, you’ll be able to save space on your device and never miss any important emails.
