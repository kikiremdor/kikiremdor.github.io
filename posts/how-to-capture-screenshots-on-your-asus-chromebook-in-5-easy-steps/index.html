The third step is to take a screen recording. This can be done by pressing ‘Ctrl + Alt + Show Windows’ at the same time. You can then draw around the area you want to record, and a timer will appear. Once you start recording, a bar at the bottom will indicate the amount of time left in your recording.

The fourth step is to open your recording in the ‘Gallery’ app on your Chromebook. You can then select the thumbnail of your recording by hovering your mouse over it. You can then choose to either edit the recording or share it with an app of your choice. Again, if you want to edit your recording, you can crop and annotate it prior to sharing.

The last step is to export both your screenshot and recording. To do this, go to the ‘Gallery’ app and select the thumbnail of your screenshot or recording. Then, select ‘Export’ and choose where you want to save your file.
