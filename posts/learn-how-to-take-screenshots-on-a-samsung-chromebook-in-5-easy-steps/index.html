Once the desired screenshot has been taken, the next step is to store the image. This can be done by pressing “Ctrl + C” to copy the image, and then pressing “Ctrl + V” to paste it into a folder or file of your choice.

Finally, you can also take screenshots of the entire Samsung Chromebook screen by pressing the “Ctrl + Shift + Show Windows” keys simultaneously. This will capture the entire screen in one image.
